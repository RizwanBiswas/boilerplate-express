Our Story

Welcome to Adda Bangla Cafe, where centuries-old Bengali culinary traditions meet contemporary gastronomy.

Nestled in the heart of the city, our Michelin-starred restaurant brings you an unparalleled dining experience that celebrates the rich heritage of Bengali cuisine. The word "Adda" represents the quintessential Bengali tradition of gathering, conversation, and connection over food.

Under the guidance of our award-winning chef, we have reimagined traditional Bengali recipes using the finest locally-sourced ingredients and innovative techniques, earning us our prestigious Michelin star.
